Delivery and payment

In our online Apple store, we do all our best to make the purchase process convenient and affordable for our customers. One of the most important aspects of this process is delivery and payment.

Delivery

We offer fast and reliable delivery of Apple products to your door. Our delivery specialists make sure that your purchase is delivered to you as soon as possible, so you can enjoy your new Apple gadgets almost immediately.

Delivery options

We offer several delivery options for your convenience. You can choose one of them making your order:

  • Courier delivery: Our couriers will bring your order directly to your home or your office. You can specify a delivery time that is convenient for you.
  • Self-pickup: If you want to pick your order up, you can select the pickup point, where your purchase will be waiting for you.

Payment

We do all our best to make the payment process for your order simple and secure. We accept various methods of payment, including:

  • Payment by credit card: You can pay for your order with credit or debit card. Your financial data is protected by the highest level of security.
  • Cash payment upon receipt: You can pay for your order in cash when you receive it from the courier.
  • Online payment: You can also use online payments for convenience.

We believe that each our clients deserves the best service, so we do all our best to provide you with fast delivery and secure payment. Thank you for choosing our online Apple store!
